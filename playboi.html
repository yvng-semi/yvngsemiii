Yvng Semi

Real Name: Prince Edris P. Bogabong

Age: 22

Address: San Isidro, General Santos, Philippines

Hobbies: Rapping, Songwriting, Playing video games, Reading light novels, manga, and manhwa

At the age of 18, Yvng Semi's musical journey commenced in 2019 making songs on his smartphone using only the Bandlab app. Drawing inspiration from artists like Juice WRLD, Lil Tecca, Lil Mosey, Polo G, Lil Tjay and a myriad of other influential artists.

Yvng Semi was raised in a home without any musical background. His experiences, whether positive or negative, are what motivates him to rap. He strives to perform to the best of his abilities every day and practices to develop his unique rapping and writing skills. Over the course of his five years in the rap scene, he has continued to get better. He composed and covered songs, but he hasn't yet put some of his original songs on Spotify since he needs to keep getting better at what he's done.
